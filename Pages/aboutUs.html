About Us

Small dreams and big plans

ESport Bife is the largest information platform on cybersports in the country.Since 2005, we have been making the small dreams and grandiose plans of millions of people come true. You can find literally everything here. We always have the latest news from the world of esports.

Our goal is to be useful

We believe that news exists to make life more interesting, pleasant and kinder. Therefore, the search for that same news should be fast, convenient and enjoyable. We don't just post the latest news from the world of e-sports. We help you find exactly what you need, in one place and without unnecessary worries, so that you don’t waste your life searching, but simply live happily. ESport Bife is a universal answer to any request, the beginning of a search and its final stop, a real assistant. We forever save our users from unpleasant compromises and make their wishes come true.
